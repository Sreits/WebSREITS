Student organization engaged in the development of New and Renewable Energy

SRE ITS exist to develop innovation, exchange ideas, and facilitate students as active learners in the Renewable Energy field. SRE ITS believes that the world's energy future will depend more on Renewable Energy.

What we do

“As a student, it’s our obligation to help the world in realizing SDGs number 7 (Affordable and Clean Energy)”

Educate ITS students about New and Renewable Energy

Facilitate technology development, basic knowledge in RE

Increase the euphoria and enthusiasm of ITS students in the Renewable Energy

What they say about being a member of SRE ITS

“SRE ITS has become a special place for me. All the things that are important as a student are fulfilled here! There are additional insights, training, upgrading soft skills, and competitions. We, as members, are given many choices that we can try to adjust to our needs which of course are very related to renewable energy. It's been an honor to be a member of SRE ITS”. - Pramudya Riandana Bhayu Gautama

“Becoming a member of ITS SRE is an opportunity to improve skills and knowledge about RE because the various trainings carried out are not boring, they are even more interesting to be listened to. And also the administrators are also friendly in accompanying members. Anyway, it's very useful starting from adding relationships because you can meet friends from other majors who also have an interest in RE and also of course increase understanding about the development of RE in the industry, both Indonesia and the world. Highly recommended for those who like RE issues!”. - Aulia Amjad Lutfi

“Really worth it!! I never lost and didn't feel burdened for the past 1 year providing time to participate in SRE ITS activities. Each event was always packaged differently and didn't make me bored, I got a lot of insight from knowledge about renewable energy itself even about public speaking!.” - Reyhan Dhihan Irawan
